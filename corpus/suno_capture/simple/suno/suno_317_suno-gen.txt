[Verse 1]
I was born where the stone walls break
On a path cut thin by the sea
Black boots on the wet green grass
And the tide kept calling me
Gulls spun white over the drop
Like notes on a restless tune
I knew your face in the foam below
And the smoke from the town at noon
[Pre-Chorus]
Oh, the wind said, "Run"
Oh, the wind said, "Stay"
My heart was a rope pulled tight
From the hill to the bay
[Chorus]
Home, home
Salt on the wind, take me home
Home, home
Cliffs in my bones, take me home
(Bring me home)
I hear it in the gulls
I hear it in my name
Home, home
Pull me back again

I’ve chased the road past breaking waves
Past all I thought I knew
But every mile just turned me round
To the shore that made me true
