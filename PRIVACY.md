# Privacy Policy — Lyric Humanizer

_Last updated: 2026-06-04_

**Short version: this app collects nothing, sends nothing, and stores nothing about you.**

Lyric Humanizer is a 100% on-device tool. You paste or type song lyrics into the app; it
scores how "AI" they read and can suggest plainer wording. Everything happens locally on
your phone.

## What it accesses
- Only the **lyrics text you type or paste into the app yourself.** Nothing else on your
  device — no contacts, location, photos, accounts, microphone, or other apps.

## What it does with that text
- The text is analyzed **entirely on your own device**, in memory, by local code shipped
  inside the app. The score and any suggestions are computed on-device.
- The text is **discarded** when you close the app or clear the box. It is not written to
  disk, backed up, or remembered between sessions.

## What it does NOT do
- **No network requests.** The app works fully offline; nothing is uploaded or sent anywhere.
- **No accounts, no sign-in, no identifiers.**
- **No tracking, analytics, cookies, fingerprinting, or advertising.**
- **No data collection, sharing, or selling** — there is nothing to share or sell.

## Permissions
- The app requests **no sensitive permissions** and does not need Internet access to function.

## Children
- The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes
- If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version
  will be posted at this URL.

## Contact
